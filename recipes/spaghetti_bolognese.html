Spaghetti Bolognese

Easy to make: Spaghetti Bolognese

Spaghetti Bolognese can not only be made quickly, but also is very delicious!

You can double or triple the ingredients, which you can store in your fridge for multiple portions.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Minced Beef
  • Sugar
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato paste

Instructions

  1. Saute garlic and onion - about 3 minutes
  2. Brown the beef - about 2 minutes
  3. Add everything else, give it a good stir then simmer for 20 minutes minimum (midweek),
    up to 3 hours (weekend indulgence!). Slow cooking makes the beef incredibly tender and the sauce develops extra flavour.
  4. Enjoy!
